About Me

My name is Nazila, and I started practicing sports with gymnastics classes at a young age. I explored various other sports later on before ultimately choosing yoga. Yoga has become a lifestyle for me, constantly changing and improving my way of living. Every day and every moment, I am learning and deepening my practice. My passion for yoga led me to obtain a certification as a yoga instructor from Yoga Alliance, and I even participated in teaching prenatal yoga to expecting mothers. I am delighted that you have chosen me to accompany you on your yoga journey. Namaste.
